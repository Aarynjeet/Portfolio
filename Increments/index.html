How to Play

Click the basketball to score points. Spend your points on upgrades to score even faster!

Upgrades

  • Better Shoes — +1 to click value per purchase. Train harder with better gear.
  • Shooting Coach — +3 to click value per purchase. A coach sharpens your shot.
  • MVP Training — +10 to click value per purchase. Elite training for elite players.
  • Team Spirit — +5 to click value per purchase. Rally the crowd behind you.
  • Ball Boy — Auto-clicker. Each purchase makes it tick faster. Only one timer runs at a time.

Rewards

  • First Basket — Score 100 total points.
  • Rising Star — Score 1,000 total points.
  • All-Star — Score 10,000 total points.
  • Rookie Contract — Purchase your first upgrade.
  • Team Player — Purchase 5 total upgrades.
  • On Autopilot — Activate the Ball Boy auto-clicker.
